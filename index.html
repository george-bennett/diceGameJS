Rules:
Player 1 rolls dice as many times as they wish, they then hold score to bank points.
Player 2 then plays the same way as Player 1.

If a player rolls a one, there score is lost and the next player takes their turn.
If a player rolls two sixes then that players banked score and round score is lost.
Player who reaches defined score first wins (or default score of 200)
